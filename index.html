Hi there! So, I'm a 38 year old bar manager. Let me take you through exactly how that came to be. I was on my way to attend class at the University of Utah on a fine and lovely September morning in 2007, when I was struck by an automobile whilst attempting to traverse a cross-walk near campus. The car was going FAST, so I was understandably pretty beat up (lots of broken stuff, hardware being implanted, etc - no need for the gory details). The rest is sort of history, as the medical debt I incurred for all the surgeries I required forced me from school and into two jobs instead. Well, it took a lot of years but here I am in this course attempting to adjust-course (no pun intended there), and perhaps find a way OUT of the bar industry which has consumed and owned me for the last 20 years or so. Feeling optimistic.
